5. name : Ratatouille
ingredients : Ingredients for 6 servings!(calories are for 1 serving)\n-eggplants 2\n-roma tomatoes 2\n-yellow squashes 2\n-zucchinis 2\n-tablespoons olive oil 2\n-onion, diced 1\n-cloves garlic, minced 4\n-red bell peppers, diced 2\n-can of crushed tomatoes 795g\n-tablespoons chopped fresh basil, from 8-10 leaves 2\n-spices(parsley, fresh, thyme, pepper, oregano)
preparation : Preheat the oven for 375˚F (190˚C).\nSlice the eggplant, tomatoes, squash, and zucchini into approximately ¹⁄₁₆-inch (1-mm) rounds, then set aside.\nMake the sauce: Heat the olive oil in a 12-inch (30-cm) oven-safe pan over medium-high heat. Sauté the onion, garlic, and bell peppers until soft, about 10 minutes. Season with salt and pepper, then add the crushed tomatoes. Stir until the ingredients are fully incorporated. Remove from heat, then add the basil. Stir once more, then smooth the surface of the sauce with a spatula.\nArrange the sliced veggies in alternating patterns, (for example, eggplant, tomato, squash, zucchini) on top of the sauce from the outer edge to the middle of the pan. Season with salt and pepper.\nMake the herb seasoning: In a small bowl, mix together the basil, garlic, parsley, thyme, salt, pepper, and olive oil. Spoon the herb seasoning over the vegetables.\nCover the pan with foil and bake for 40 minutes. Uncover, then bake for another 20 minutes, until the vegetables are softened.
calories : 306
category : lunch

6. name : Fish Stew
ingredients : Ingredients for 4 servings!(calories are for 1 serving)\n-slices stale bread , diced 4\n-tbsp olive oil 2\n-onion , finely chopped 1\n-garlic cloves , crushed 2\n-tsp dried chilli flakes 1\n-can chopped tomatoes 400\n-frozen white fish fillets, such as cod or pollock 4\n-can butter beans , drained 400g\n-small pack parsley , roughly chopped\n-1 lemon , cut into wedges
preparation : Heat oven to 200C/180C fan/gas 6. Put the bread on a large baking sheet, drizzle over 1 tbsp oil and bake for 10 mins until golden. Set aside.\nMeanwhile, heat the rest of the oil in a large flameproof casserole dish over a medium heat.\nAdd the onion and cook until softened for about 10 mins, then add the garlic and chilli flakes and stir for 1 min. Tip in the tomatoes and fish fillets. Cover and simmer for 10 mins until the fish is nearly cooked, then uncover.\nTip in the butter beans, season well, then cook until everything is hot. Serve scattered with the croutons, parsley and lemon.
calories : 325
category : lunch
